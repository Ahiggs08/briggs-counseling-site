About Shawn

You Don't Have to Face It Alone

I help people through their healing and growth process by creating a space where they feel seen and heard — a place to feel and speak freely. Whether you're an individual or a couple, my goal is to get to the root of the matter and figure out how to rebuild.

This requires looking inside ourselves to create self-awareness and make meaning of the suffering we've been through. Understanding life's meaning and one's purpose creates a foundation for lasting growth.

"My goal is to help you remove the roadblocks that are preventing you from healing and growth — through sincere connection and exploration of self."

Therapeutic Approach

How We'll Work Together

I draw from multiple evidence-based approaches, tailored to your unique needs and goals.

Cognitive Behavioral (CBT)

Identify and reshape thought patterns that contribute to emotional distress. Build practical tools for managing anxiety and depression.

Attachment-Based

Explore how early relationships shape current patterns. Heal relational wounds and build healthier connections with others.

Existential Therapy

Find deeper meaning and purpose in your experiences. Develop self-awareness and make sense of life's challenges.

Family Systems

Understand your role within family dynamics. Improve communication and strengthen relationships across generations.

Acceptance & Commitment

Learn psychological flexibility. Accept difficult emotions while committing to actions aligned with your values.

Person-Centered

You are the expert on your own life. I provide unconditional positive regard and a genuinely empathetic partnership.

Areas of Focus

How I Can Help

Specialized support for a wide range of concerns, tailored to where you are in life.

Grief & Loss

Navigate the pain of loss with someone who understands. Process grief at your own pace and find a path toward acceptance and meaning.

Learn to manage overwhelming worry and find calm. Develop practical coping strategies that work in your daily life.

Strengthen your relationship, improve communication, and work through conflict together. Build a stronger partnership for the future.
