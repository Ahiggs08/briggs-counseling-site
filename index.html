About Shawn

You Don't Have to Face It Alone

My approach is centered on empathy and collaboration, allowing each person's unique experiences and goals to guide the work we do together. Rather than using a one-size-fits-all method, I draw from a variety of therapeutic approaches to create a plan that fits each individual.

My goal is to help clients make meaningful progress by addressing current struggles and by exploring the deeper patterns that may be holding them back from living a more balanced and fulfilling life. I have witnessed how beauty and growth can emerge from life's suffering, and it is my deepest hope that all of my clients experience that same transformation.

Outside of my work, I value time with my family and I have a love of learning for both people and processes. I enjoy activities like hiking, camping, cooking, and eating good food.

"The most important teacher of the meaning in your life is your suffering." — Arthur Brooks

Therapeutic Approach

How We'll Work Together

I draw from multiple evidence-based approaches, tailored to your unique needs and goals.

Cognitive Behavioral (CBT)

Identify and reshape thought patterns that contribute to emotional distress. Build practical tools for managing anxiety and depression.

Attachment-Based

Explore how early relationships shape current patterns. Heal relational wounds and build healthier connections with others.

Existential Therapy

Find deeper meaning and purpose in your experiences. Develop self-awareness and make sense of life's challenges.

Family Systems

Understand your role within family dynamics. Improve communication and strengthen relationships across generations.

Acceptance & Commitment

Learn psychological flexibility. Accept difficult emotions while committing to actions aligned with your values.

Person-Centered

You are the expert on your own life. I provide unconditional positive regard and a genuinely empathetic partnership.
